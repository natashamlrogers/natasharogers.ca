About the Series

Where Women Come to Learn,
Connect & Feel Empowered

YYC Girls Night University is Calgary's growing community of women who show up curious and leave more confident. Inspired by the success of our Investing 101 launch which brought 46 women together at Two Rivers Distillery we're expanding into a three-part series covering the financial topics women want to talk about most.

Each evening is moderated, conversational, and anything but a boring seminar. Think drinks, great people, and an expert in the room making intimidating topics finally feel accessible.
